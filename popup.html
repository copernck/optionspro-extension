Quick Glossary

Call Option
Right to buy underlying asset at strike price
Put Option
Right to sell underlying asset at strike price
Strike Price
Price at which option can be exercised
Expiration
Date when option contract expires
Premium
Price paid for option contract
ITM/OTM
In-the-money / Out-of-the-money
